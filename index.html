The Creative

I'm Kyle Christopher — a visual creative based in metro Atlanta, telling stories through a lens.

Known across the platforms as @chrisfilmed and CHRISUNCUT, I shoot and edit cinematic content with a heavy focus on sports — capturing the intensity of game day, the grind of practice, and the moments athletes want to remember forever.

From basketball highlight reels and field-side coverage to lifestyle, events and automotive visuals, my work lives at the intersection of photography and motion. Every frame is built to feel like a film, not just footage.
